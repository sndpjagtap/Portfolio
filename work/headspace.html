Headspace

Animation for the world's leading mindfulness and meditation app

< All projects

Headspace is all about making meditation simple and accessible. It's both app and web based, so you can learn online, when you want, wherever you are.

When they got in touch earlier this year to see if I would be interested in putting together some infinitely looping animations for their social feeds, I jumped on it.

It felt like a double win doing this project, because there's real value in a product that helps people and it was also an opportunity for fun, creative work.

The animations support mindfulness advice — reminders for us to make time for ourselves and others.

This project came about as a direct result of my animation side project, Moving which feels really good — you never know who is watching.

Ok, that sounds really creepy, but you know what I mean.
